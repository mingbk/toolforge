💡 Tips:
• 2048-bit keys are sufficient for most use cases; 4096-bit offers more security but slower generation
• RSA encryption has a length limit: 2048-bit keys encrypt up to ~190 bytes; 4096-bit up to ~446 bytes
• To encrypt long messages, use AES to encrypt the content and RSA to encrypt the AES key
• All computation happens locally in your browser. Keys are never uploaded.
